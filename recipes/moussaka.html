Moussaka Recipe

Misaka'a as it's called in Egypt

peel them, slice and salt them, then lay them in single layers in a tray,

Even though I didn't like eggplant as a child, I was fascinated by this process. My mom explained that eggplant was as absorbent as a sponge, and if she didn't dehydrate them a little through the salt drawing out the water, they'd soak up too much oil in the frying process.

Ingredients

  • onion
  • garlic
  • cinnamon
  • eggplant slices

Steps

  1. She'd peel them, slice
  2. salt them,
  3. lay them in single layers in a tray
