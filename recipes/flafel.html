Flafel Recipe

Falafel's a fave!

We're big fans of these deep-fried chickpea patties. We love ours served in the classic style, stuffed into an envelope of pita bread with all the traditional falafel fixings -- sliced cucumbers, chopped lettuce and tomato, and, of course, a big splash of tahini sauce. Often we'll pop in some pickles, too! The falafel sandwich makes for a filling lunch, for sure. But we don't stop there.

Ingredients

  • Dried chickpeas
  • Fresh herbs
  • Onion
  • Baking powder
  • Sesame seeds
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Soak chickpeas for 24 hours.
  2. Make mixture.
  3. Refrigerate (important.)
  4. Make mixture.
  5. Form patties or balls.
  6. Fry.
